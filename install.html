◇ FAQ

Is this really free?
Yes. Spark Verse is completely free. Premium features and expanded tools are coming soon at thefirstspark.shop for those who want to go deeper.
Will my data sync across devices?
Not currently. Your data is stored locally on each device. Cloud sync is coming in a future update for premium members.
Why does it look different in the browser vs installed?
When installed as an app, it runs in "standalone" mode without browser navigation bars - giving you the full immersive experience. That's why we recommend installing it.
I lost my data. Can you recover it?
Unfortunately no. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we never see it, we can't recover it. Clearing browser data or uninstalling will erase your history.
How do I get updates?
The app updates automatically when you open it and have an internet connection. The service worker will fetch the latest version.
